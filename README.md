# 🍺 BeerRate - Beer Rating App

A simple web application to rate and track your favorite beers. Perfect for beer enthusiasts who want to remember which beers they've tried and how much they enjoyed them.

## Features

- **Add Beer Ratings**: Enter beer name, brewery, style, and rate from 1-3 stars
- **Search Functionality**: Find beers by name, brewery, or style
- **Local Storage**: All data is saved in your browser (no internet required)
- **Responsive Design**: Works on desktop and mobile devices
- **Beer Styles**: Choose from 12 common beer styles
- **Delete Ratings**: Remove beers you no longer want to track

## How to Use

1. **Open the App**: Double-click on `index.html` to open it in your web browser
2. **Add a Beer**: 
   - Fill in the beer name and brewery
   - Select a beer style from the dropdown
   - Click one of the rating buttons (1-3 stars)
   - Click "Add Beer"
3. **Search**: Use the search box to find specific beers
4. **Delete**: Click the delete button on any beer to remove it

## Beer Rating Scale

- ⭐ **1 Star - Okay**: Drinkable but not memorable
- ⭐⭐ **2 Stars - Good**: Enjoyable, would drink again
- ⭐⭐⭐ **3 Stars - Excellent**: Outstanding, highly recommended

## Browser Storage Note

Your beer ratings are saved in your browser's local storage. This means:
- ✅ Data persists when you close/reopen the browser
- ✅ No internet connection required
- ⚠️ Data is tied to this specific browser
- ⚠️ Clearing browser data will delete your ratings
- ⚠️ Data won't sync between different browsers/devices
